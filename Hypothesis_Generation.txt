Hypothesis generation is the process of listing out all the possible factors that can affect the outcome.
It involves understanding the problem in detail by brainstorming as many factors as possible which can impact the outcome. It is done by understanding the problem statement properly and before looking at the data.

Some of the factors that can affect Loan Approval decision: -

Salary: - Applicants with high income should have more chances of loan approval.

Previous history: - Applicants who have repayed their previous debts should have higher chances of loan approval.

Loan amount: - If the loan amount is less, chances of loan approval will be high.

Loan term: - Loan for lesser time period will have higher chances of approval.

EMI: - Lesser the amount to be repaid monthly, higher chances of approval.
